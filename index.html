Tilted's Features.

Web-Based

Tilted is web-based and hosted in the cloud. No downloads and its always ready. On your PC or on the go with your mobile.

Dashboard

With our minimalistic and modern user interface and dashboard, you can dive right in and control Tilted Bot with comfort and ease.

Free

Our bot is completley free. No ads or pressured premium services. No tricks or gimmicks. Just you and Tilted.

Customizability

We offer you the ability to cusomize your experience. You can set Tilted up the way you want, and even make custom commands.

Community

Our core features, like giveaways, timers, chat control, and more, help you build your community. We help you control and build.

Open Source

Tilted is completley open souce. We want to share our bot with the world. We welcome intergrations and people building upon our bot.

Why Tilted?

No cost!

Tilted is 100% free. There are no pushy premium services, gimmicks, or annoying advertisements. Just complete access to our tool to control and build your community.

Simple.

With our modern and minimalistic dashboard and user interface, Tilted is a breeze to use. This lets first time users dive right in and get started.

Efficient.

Since Tilted is so simple, that means it is more efficient. You can set the bot up quickly and to fit your needs, which means more time to concentrate on your stream, and less time configuring.

Development & API.

Open Source and Community Driven Development.

Tilted is based on Twitch API and TMI.js and powered by node.js and json. Our front end design was built with html5, css3, jquery, and bootstrap 3. Our work is a open source web appliation that we want to share with the world. We want people to take what we have and build upon it to make twitch solutions even better. We will continue to add features in the future and we also plan to implement an extensive API for those who would like to intergrate with Tilted.

Getting Started:

1. Sign up

Click the sign up button and enter your information and click the "connect with twitch" button. This syncs your Tilted account with twitch.

2. Join Chat

After signing up and logging in, enter your settings and then have tilted join your chat by toggling the "Tilted Join" switch.

3. Stream On

Once Tilted has joined your chat, feel free to use the features on dashboard set back and let Tilted do all the work. Stream on!
